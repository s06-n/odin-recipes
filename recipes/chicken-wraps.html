Chicken wraps

In Need Of A Quick And Tasty Lunch

This chicken wrap is succulent and easy to make, just what you need on a dreary Tuesday morning. The warmth of the spices used will bring back some of the warmth that was stolen by the weather. Also, reat cooled down in the usummer with a nic cold beverage.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon paprika powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 lime
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 500g chicken breast
  • a cup of your favourite vegetable mix
  • 1/3 of a cabbage
  • 2 avocados
  • a tin of sweetcorn

Steps

  1. Prep your chicken breast by cutting it into small cubes and set aside, if it is frozen dethaw hours in advance.
  2. Then, mix all of the spices, squeeze the lime and add the olive oil and mix. This is your marinade.
  3. Coat your chicken in the marinade and set aside for atleast 30 minutes upto overnight.
  4. Get your vegetable mix and thinly sliced cabbage, and put it in a pan with enough water for it to be submerged. Season with 2 teaspoons salt and 1 teaspoon coriander powder. You should boil until the vegatables are soft, approximately an hour or whenever the water dissapears.
  5. Get your tin of sweetcorn and heat up and season with sallt to taste for about 10 minutes.
  6. Prep avocado and blend, season with salt to taste.
  7. Get chicken out of the fridge and cook on a pan for 4-5 minutes on both sides.
  8. Shred chicken on a plastic cutting board.
  9. Assemble wrap: spread mayonnaise, then add chicken, vegetables, avocado, sauces of your choice and wrap.
  10. Voila, you now have a tasty meal, very easy to make and is perfect for meal prepping.
