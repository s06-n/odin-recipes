Fish And Mash

In Need Of A Quick And Tasty Dinner?

This fish and mash is just what you need when you want a healthy, hearty dinner. You will definitely make this recipe more than once, especially on those colder days, when you are in need of a hearty meal. Fee free to customise and to add vegetables to the mix for a pop of colour and flavour.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 lime
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tilapia fish
  • 3 large potatoes

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180C and dethaw the fish and set aside.
  2. Peel potatoes and cut into small cubes and boil, this will speed up the cooking process.
  3. Season fish and put in a baking glass/tray and put into oven for 30 minutes.
  4. Drain potatoes of water only leaving a little and mash, adding salt, mayonnaise, milk and cheese.
  5. Plate your fish and mash and enjoy!
